A, B, and C—    worms are blind and can't see.
D, E, and F—    the cats ran, none are left.
G, H, and I—  birds fly high in the sky.
J, K, and L—    a pig's stinky feet smell.
M, N, and O—    polar bears sleep in snow.
P, Q, and R—    whales can swim far.
S, T, and U—  bluebirds are blue.
V, W, and X—   what comes next?
Y and Z—      say your letters like me!
